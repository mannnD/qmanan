Query

If you were asked to play a 20 round coin toss with your friend then what will be the probability of your winning? I guess the answer is as simple as 0.5 and you did this with the conventional method of calculating probability  p=(number of favorable events)/(total possible events. Ok now we will play this game with a normal computer and again as you think the theoretical probability will be 0.5 and the practical probability will also be nearby 0.5.

Probability is the measure of uncertainty of any event (any phenomenon happened or bound to happen). On what parameters the probability works are generally the occurrence of an event and the way it occurs. It has been a great tool in statistical data analysis and many emerging technologies such as machine learning, deep learning, artificial intelligence and quantum computing.  

The experiment

Shohini Ghose is a multi-award-winning quantum physicist. In one of her ted talks she  did an experiment by playing the coin game with the audience but here the opponent was a Quantum computer. It starts with a head and the computer has a chance to either  flip the coin or not and the outcome is not revealed to you and then you will get the same choice to flip the coin or not and the outcome isn’t shown to the computer and at last step the computer again has a choice. If the head comes at the end the computer wins. And yes it wins  97% times, and it becomes possible by  quantum computing.

How quantum computing works?

The computer which was used in this game was a quantum computer developed by IBM.  Quantum computers and computers differ from each other as much as the car is different from carpet, not a sarcasm but reality. Our conventional computers work on the binary  codes.

                   HEAD(0)                                          TAIL(1)

   

Humans program in “high level” languages which are closer to human languages. But computers don’t speak high level, they only understand binary code — whether something is 1 or 0, on or off — so it has to get translated from high to low level. On the other hand quantum computers are based on the principles of quantum physics. A  normal computer converts the head coin input as 1 and the tail coin input as 0. So due to discrete bits of 0 and 1 it can only conclude  that the coin can either possibly come on the head side or tail side. As a result the win ratio of computer comes nearly  0.5.

But the computation technique in case of quantum computers becomes a little bit complex.  Quantum physics describes the behavior of atoms and other fundamental particles like electrons, photons etc. And so a quantum computer operates by controlling the behavior of these particles, the very fundamental properties of these particles and on the behalf of them it computes the possibilities of happening or the solution of a problem or something that sounds fictional. Differ from normal computers the quantum computer creates a non binary fluid identity by the superposition principle.

NON binary fluid

This non binary fluid identity somewhere lies between 1 and 0 or better say between the head and tail and this is stored in a quantum bit very different from a binary bit. During the game we do not stick on the precise o and 1 and instead of that we allow some uncertainty in this by using heisenberg’s principle of uncertainty.

Why go with quantum?

For decades we have been working on decreasing the size of a machine and increasing its speed and when we see it in future it turns out as quantum computers. Nowadays banking, E-commerce and research and  from marketing to medication all are becoming digital so we can see the use of quantum in any of the fields. Yes it is possible,  the cure of aids and its vaccine have been a big problem for many years but in future it can be easily made by using quantum since it works on much fundamental level of particles.The enhancement of crypto-currency and blockchain technology will be powered by quantum computing. With its help we will  be able to design much efficient security systems and can implement them. Their system designs will be unpredictable because of quantum uncertainty and hence it will be impossible to hack them.The probability of a particle existing at some place is non zero and this principle could be used as information teleportation technology.
